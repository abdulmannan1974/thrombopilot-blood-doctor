# CHADS2 Score for Atrial Fibrillation Stroke Risk

**Source**: Thrombosis Canada — https://thrombosiscanada.ca/tools/?calc=chads2
**Type**: Scoring Calculator
**Scraped**: 2026-03-12

---

## Overview

Estimates stroke risk in patients with non-valvular atrial fibrillation using the CHADS2 criteria.

---

## Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| **C** — Congestive Heart Failure history | **+1** |
| **H** — Hypertension history | **+1** |
| **A** — Age ≥ 75 | **+1** |
| **D** — Diabetes Mellitus history | **+1** |
| **S2** — Previous Stroke or TIA | **+2** |

**Maximum Score**: 6

---

## Score Interpretation — Annual Stroke Risk

| CHADS2 Score | Adjusted Stroke Rate (%/year) | Risk Category |
|--------------|-------------------------------|---------------|
| 0 | 1.9% | Low |
| 1 | 2.8% | Low–Moderate |
| 2 | 4.0% | Moderate |
| 3 | 5.9% | Moderate–High |
| 4 | 8.5% | High |
| 5 | 12.5% | High |
| 6 | 18.2% | Very High |

---

## Clinical Application

| Score | Recommendation |
|-------|----------------|
| 0 | ASA or no therapy |
| 1 | OAC (preferred) or ASA |
| ≥ 2 | OAC recommended |

*Note: CHADS2 has been largely superseded by CHA2DS2-VASc in current guidelines, which provides better discrimination at the lower end of risk.*
